## Notes

- EASY (`golden_set.jsonl`, 30 distinctive questions) is saturated at 1.0 —
  expected, since each question names its source study distinctively.
- HARD (`golden_hard.jsonl`, paraphrased questions) is where there's room to
  move: hit@1=0.967 means one question out of 30 doesn't rank the correct DOI
  first (it still lands in top-3, since hit@3=1.0). No HARD question misses
  the top-5 entirely (hit@5=1.0), so `eval_retrieval`'s `misses` list is empty
  for this run.
- Known caveat carried from Phase A: hard-set Q8 is under-specified (fits two
  food-decision studies) — this is the likely source of the HARD hit@1 gap.
  If Phase B hybrid retrieval moves this number, check whether Q8 specifically
  flipped before crediting the retriever.
- REFUSAL is measured on 10 all-obvious off-topic questions
  (`golden_refuse.jsonl`) — not yet adversarial near-misses.
